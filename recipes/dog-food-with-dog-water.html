Dry dog food with dog water

Oooweee

If you need a nice night in with a great easy homemade meal then look no further. This recipe will have you eating some of the most nutritious and delicious food you've ever had!

You'll need a few things:

  • 2 cups dry dog food (Use whatever your favorite brand or mix may be)
  • 2 cups wet dog water (You want it to be wet but not overly so)
  • A container large enough to put them in (Stainless steel over plastic every time)

Here's what you'll need to do with them:

  1. Fill the first container with the dry dog food
  2. Fill the second container with the wet dog water (It's crucial that the two do not mix)
  3. Chow down!
