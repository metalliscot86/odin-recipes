Peanut Butter Excavation

It's time to work

You're sitting there coding away, making a practice website so you can hopefully start a career and a life that doesn't make you miserable. It's the middle of the night and you get that little cramp in your stomach. You gotta eat... and here's a recipe that'll keep you firmly planted in that chair so you can keep typing away.

All you need is 2 things

  • A big spoon that can still fit in your mouth
  • A bigger jar of peanut butter

THE RECIPE

  1. Open the jar
  2. Excavate it in your mouth
  3. Keep typing
